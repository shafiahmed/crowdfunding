Crowdfunding is the practice of funding a project or cause by raising money from a large number of people, typically through the Internet. It is a form of peer-to-peer fundraising that harnesses the power of social networks to raise awareness and draw donations from around the world for online campaigns.



How to Crowdfund

CREATE

Ibtida’s easy-to-use fundraiser template allows you to create an effective home page for your crowdfunding campaign. Show potential donors how they can help by telling a compelling story, uploading several pictures and videos, and posting updates.

SHARE

Activate your social network. With just a few clicks, spread the word of your fundraiser to friends, family and other potential donors through Facebook, Linkedin, email and other channels.

COLLECT

Donations to your fundraiser can be transferred into your account as they’re received. Ibtida provides free fundraising, which means we don’t charge a thing. The donation processor does, however, take 5%, plus a nominal amount per transaction.
